Game Rules

The rules are a bit different from the actual hardball game.


Fielding Regulations

  • Depends on where we play and availability of space - not easy finding a ground in NYC dedicated for tape-ball cricket. BUT THAT IS ABOUT TO CHANGE!

  • During powerplay, only 3 allowed outside of circle.


What counts as out!

  1. Catch
  2. Runout!
  3. Hit Wicket
  4. Obstructing the field

Team Selection

  • Maximum Players in a squad: 12
  • Total allowed to play a match: 9
  • Minimum number of players required to play a match: 7
  • Must pick at least 4 batsmen, 3-4 allrounders, 3-4 bowlers, and one wicket keeper.
  • During the match, all teams must have atleast 2 batsman, 2 allrounders, 2 bowlers, and one wicketkeeper.

Punctuality

Warning - all players must arrive 30 minutes before start of play.

Final team for the match to be handed to umpires 15 minutes before match start. No Excuses.

If team does not have minimum number of players required to play match, the team in question must pay fine in order to continue participating in tournament.


Equipment Allocation

Besides team jerseys, equipment to be provided to all teams!

All tournaments will be shirt color coded with each team.
